About us

Having worked in the advertising services market for many years, we faced quite various challenges in dealing with network retail outlets every day. We managed to overcome all these difficulties while remaining responsible and delivering high-quality performance.

We have devoted a lot of time to building strong relationship with the staff, even in the most remote and small retail outlets all across world.

By being present daily on all federal and regional networks, we closely monitor any changes in the market. This allows us to respond promptly to the happenings, as well as, to follow new trends.

Our mission

  • To provide services of the highest quality even in the most remote parts of the world.
  • To demonstrate total transparency in all business operations. Our clients know exactly what work they are paying for and can check it everything thoroughly.
  • To provide services accessible to business of any size - whether it is a small enterprise or a large manufacturer.
  • To bear full social responsibility towards all our employees by providing them with the guarantees of timely and fair remuneration.
